Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 13, 2026

What is Enclave?

Enclave is a private, invite-only workspace for business interruption consulting and network management. Access is restricted to users explicitly added to the allowlist by an administrator.

Google Account Data

Enclave uses Google Sign-In to authenticate members. When you sign in, we receive your name, email address, and profile photo from your Google account. This information is used solely to identify you within the community.

Google Drive Integration

Enclave uses the Google Picker API to let you attach files from your own Google Drive to posts and projects. We request only the drive.file scope, which means:

  • Enclave can only access the specific files you select through the Picker.
  • Enclave cannot browse, read, or list any other files in your Drive.
  • Enclave does not download, copy, or store the contents of your Drive files. We store only the file link, file name, and icon URL so the attachment can be displayed.

Data Storage

Enclave stores community data (posts, messages, events, projects, tasks) in Google Firebase (Firestore). This data is private to your Enclave community and is not shared with or sold to any third party.

Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not use analytics trackers or advertising pixels.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.
  • We do not retain Google Drive data beyond the metadata of files you explicitly attach.

Data Deletion

If you are removed from the allowlist, your access is revoked immediately. To request deletion of your stored data, contact the administrator at the email used to invite you.
